Course Outline

The beauty of this training is that it can be done online, by people of all abilities, and age.

Month 1: Building the Foundation

Learn to overcome tone deafness through ear training and familiarize yourself with tonic solfa while practicing daily vocal and breath control exercises.

Month 2: Finding Your Singing Voice

Develop your unique vocal style with advanced breath control techniques and continued ear training for better pitch accuracy.

Month 3: Expanding Your Range

Master vocal registers, take a vocal range test, and learn techniques to effectively extend and control your vocal range.

Month 4: Mastering Harmony & Arrangements

Understand harmony arrangement fundamentals, refine ear training, and apply advanced harmony techniques to enhance vocal performance.
